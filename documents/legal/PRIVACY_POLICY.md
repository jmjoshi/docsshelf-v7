# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 26, 2025**

## Introduction

DocsShelf ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the DocsShelf mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, password (encrypted), first name, last name, phone numbers
- **Documents**: Files you upload, including images, PDFs, and metadata (filename, date, category, tags)
- **Usage Data**: App preferences, settings, backup history

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data**: App crashes, error logs, performance metrics
- **Storage Usage**: Local storage consumption for documents and database

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide Core Services**: Store, organize, and manage your documents securely
- **Account Management**: Create and maintain your account, authenticate access
- **Security**: Implement MFA, biometric authentication, account lockout protection
- **Backup & Restore**: Create encrypted backups and restore your data
- **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, optimize performance
- **Communication**: Send important notifications about your account and documents

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **All data is stored locally** on your device using SQLite database with SQLCipher encryption
- **Documents** are stored in your device's secure file system
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption for database, PBKDF2 for password hashing
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your data on our servers (unless you explicitly use cloud backup features)

### Security Measures

- **Encryption at Rest**: Database and sensitive data encrypted with industry-standard algorithms
- **Secure Password Storage**: PBKDF2 hashing with salt (100,000 iterations)
- **Multi-Factor Authentication**: Optional TOTP-based MFA for additional security
- **Biometric Authentication**: Optional Face ID/Touch ID support
- **Auto-Lock**: Configurable session timeout for device security
- **Account Lockout**: Protection against brute-force attacks (5 attempts, 15-minute lockout)

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes
- **Security & Fraud Prevention**: To protect against security threats or fraudulent activity
- **Business Transfer**: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

### Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for:

- **Analytics**: Crash reporting and performance monitoring (optional, can be disabled)
- **Cloud Backup**: If you enable cloud backup, data is encrypted before transmission

We ensure third-party services comply with privacy standards and data protection regulations.

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: View all data stored in the App
- **Export Your Data**: Create backups and export documents at any time
- **Delete Your Data**: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics, notifications, and optional features
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in standard formats (.docsshelf backup format)

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:
1. Use the in-app settings (Profile, Security, Preferences)
2. Create a backup before deleting data
3. Contact us at support@docsshelf.com for assistance

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data retained for 1 year of inactivity, then deleted
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backup Files**: Retained until you manually delete them from your device

## Children's Privacy

DocsShelf is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

If you use DocsShelf outside your country of residence, your data may be transferred to and processed in different jurisdictions. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.
